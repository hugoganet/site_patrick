about us

PPS is a Paris-based studio founded in 2024 by Patrick Paleta, a creative director and consultant specializing in brand identities. After co-founding Chevalvert in 2007, Patrick launched PPS to continue his vision of thoughtful and impactful design.
The studio's approach is defined by a methodical practice, placing collaboration at the heart of every project and keeping the target audience at the center of its focus. Serving both institutional and commercial clients, PPS delivers graphic solutions that are rigorous, aesthetically refined, and timeless.
